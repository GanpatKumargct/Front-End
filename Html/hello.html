To understand 
            the significance of Attlee's assertion, 
            we have to go back in time to 1945. 
            The Second World War had ended. 
            The allied powers led by Britain and the United States had won. 
            The axis powers led by Hitler's Germany had been vanquished. The victors wanted to impose justice on the defeated armies. 
            In India, officers of Netaji Bose's Indian National Army were put on trial for treason, torture, murder. 
            This series of court martials, came to be known as the Red Fort Trials.
